Service Fabric Chaos

What's this?

Large-scale distributed systems like cloud infrastructures are inherently unreliable. Azure Service Fabric enables you to write reliable distributed services on top of an unreliable infrastructure. To write verifiably robust distributed services on top of an unreliable infrastructure, you need to be able to test the stability of your services while the underlying unreliable infrastructure is going through complicated state transitions due to faults. This is where Chaos comes in: Service Fabric Chaos provides a very simple way to induce targeted, simulated, realistic faults that can help identify bugs that show up only in hard-to-predict, long, and complicated sequences of state transitions.

Click Stop Chaos to immediately end your Chaos experiment.
